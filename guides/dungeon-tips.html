Dungeon Tips

A short field note for safer dungeon clears and less wasted preparation.

Last updated July 1, 2026

Quick rule

Enter with a plan. If your build cannot recover from one bad floor, choose a safer mode or adjust gear first.

Prepare before entering

Check supplies, swap out risky farming gear, and make sure your defensive skills are ready.

Keep Ward in rotation

Dungeon losses often come from one nasty turn. Ward gives you room to recover.

Respect your keys

Do not spend rare keys proving a build is not ready. Test safer content first.
