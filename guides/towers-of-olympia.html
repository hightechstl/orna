Towers of Olympia Guide

A practical tower planning guide for Orna and Hero of Aethric: keys, locations, shard banking, Titan Workshop choices, celestial weapons, and personal towers.

Last updated July 1, 2026T9+ endgameTower Shards

Quick verdict

Towers of Olympia are T9+ solo endgame content. They are one of the main ways to earn Tower Shards, build toward celestial weapons, collect Titan augments, and unlock celestial classes.

Treat towers like long risk-management runs. The best tower player knows when to bank shards and leave.

What Towers are

Towers are longform solo content. You climb floors, defeat enemies, collect Tower Shards, and try to reach the Titan boss at the top.

Tower Shards are spent through the Titan Workshop on tower upgrades, celestial weapons, augments, and celestial classes. Orna and Hero of Aethric share many mechanics, but locations and access differ.

Starting the tower quest

Pelops starts the Towers of Olympia quest and gives your first Olympia Tower Key. In Orna, Pelops appears like other quest NPCs. In Hero of Aethric, Pelops is in New Lyonesse.

If Pelops is missing

Clear outstanding side quests and check again before assuming something is broken.

Getting Olympia Tower Keys

Tower Guardian is the key monster to farm. Tower Guardians are T9 world spawns, and Olympia Tower Keys should not be spent casually when your build is unstable.

  • Use luck and monster-spawn boosting tools where appropriate.
  • Affinity candles can help filter toward higher-tier enemies.
  • Tower floors can sometimes reward keys.
  • Bazaars may rarely sell them.
  • The Runeshop may be an option for players who choose to use it.

Verify drop behavior

Exact drop behavior, spawn methods, and shop availability can change. Check current in-game behavior before building a routine around old notes.

Finding towers

Orna

  • Towers spawn randomly in the world.
  • Use Scouthawk from the Keep, Fortress, or Castle Lookout.
  • Turn on the compass/buildings map layer.
  • Towers shuffle locations weekly.
  • Towers do not replace dungeon spawns.
  • Wayvessel interaction may be restricted; verify current behavior in-game.

Tower gameplay basics

  • Towers cost 1 Olympia Tower Key per entry.
  • Re-entering the same tower has a cooldown.
  • Different towers do not necessarily share cooldowns like dungeons.
  • Each floor has Floor Guardians and stray monsters.
  • Killing one Floor Guardian lets you ascend.
  • Clearing more enemies can increase Tower Shard gains, but increases risk.
  • Higher floors are more rewarding and more dangerous.
  • Stray monsters give less shard value than Floor Guardians.
  • Tower chests can give buffs and puzzle keys.
  • If you die, you lose a portion of the Tower Shards earned in that run.
  • Exiting safely lets you keep earned shards but prevents reaching the Titan boss.
  • The Titan boss is on the final floor and can drop augments.

Tower strategy

  • Do not greed every floor if your build is unstable.
  • Decide whether the goal is shard farming or Titan completion.
  • Change from view-distance gear to combat gear after entering.
  • Prioritize survival over speed.
  • Avoid horde fights that counter your build.
  • For Summoner: establish opening summons, maintain Ward, and do not sacrifice summons carelessly.
  • For fragile classes: take chests, avoid unnecessary fights, and leave safely if the run is going bad.

Wild tower growth and resets

Wild towers grow over time. More floors generally means better shard potential but harder enemies. Archived guide material listed towers growing from 15 to 50 floors and gaining floors throughout the week.

Check current schedules

Exact growth timing and reset schedules may have changed. Verify with current community tools, Discord bots, or in-game observation.

Titan Workshop

The Titan Workshop is where Tower Shards are spent. Orna players can build it in the Origin Town after starting the quest. Hero of Aethric has workshop access through game areas depending on tier and progression.

Celestial weapons and augments

Celestial weapons are built and upgraded through the workshop. Titan augments come from Titan bosses and are for celestial weapons. Celestial weapons use augment slots, not regular adornment logic.

Check current costs

Shard costs can become stale. Check current sheets or in-game values before committing.

Personal towers

Personal towers are built by the player, do not move like wild towers, and can be upgraded. You can only build one of each tower theme.

Shard planning rule

Do not spend shards randomly. Decide whether your next goal is a celestial class, celestial weapon, personal tower, or weapon upgrade.

Best early tower goals

  1. Learn which tower fights are safe for your class.
  2. Survive and bank shards.
  3. Build toward the celestial class that supports your main build.
  4. Work on celestial weapon progression.
  5. Build personal towers only when you understand the shard cost and long-term value.

Common tower mistakes

  • Entering with view-distance gear and forgetting to swap.
  • Greeding every fight.
  • Fighting hordes that counter your build.
  • Ignoring tower chests.
  • Dying instead of exiting safely.
  • Spending shards without a plan.
  • Assuming old tower reset/floor schedules are still perfectly accurate.
