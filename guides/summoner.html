Summoner Guide

A practical route through Attuner and Summoner with enough survival, summon power, and pact discipline to keep progress smooth.

Last updated July 1, 2026Class guideT7-T10

Quick verdict

For most players, the best long-term route is Attuner → Grand Attuner → Summoner → Grand Summoner → Grand Summoner Auriga.

Hydrus can work, but Auriga is the safer default for the summon-first playstyle most players expect from the class line.

What Summoner does

Summoner is not just a normal mage with a different spell list. The player character is fragile, and summons carry most of the fight. Your job is to survive, keep Ward online, set up the summon field, and use pacts intelligently.

Summoners also do not use followers like normal pet classes. Your power budget is tied to summoned allies and pact timing, so do not evaluate the line like Beowulf-style pet play.

Unlocking

The line starts at T7 Attuner, which is unlocked through Horus. Later classes are purchased as you reach the correct tiers. Verify exact orn costs in-game because older guide costs can become outdated after balance or progression changes.

Celestial choices

Grand Summoner Auriga

Auriga is the default summon-focused endgame choice. Pick it for the most straightforward summon-first route.

Grand Summoner Hydrus

Hydrus is the pact and sacrifice niche. It can hit hard, but it asks more from your setup and is easier to pilot badly.

Tower Shards matter

Celestial class progression uses Towers of Olympia and Tower Shards. Read the Towers of Olympia guide before spending shards.

Build priorities

  • Summon Stats
    Boost the units that carry your damage.
  • Ward
    Your most important survival layer.
  • HP
    More room for bad turns.
  • Resistance/Defense
    Stop one damage type from folding you.
  • Status Immunity
    Prevents lost turns and lockouts.
  • Instant Summon / summon start chance
    Helps stabilize before enemies punish you.

Recommended specialization

Benefactor is the general summon-scaling recommendation. Time Mage or Chronomancer can help with Stasis/control while leveling or undergeared.

Survivability matters more than looking strong on paper. A setup that dies before the field is ready is not a damage build.

Gear priorities

Prioritize Summoner/Attuner gear, Charon staff variants, Spiritgarm Grimoire variants, Omega Riftlock, and Baldr gear for survivability. Swap to Ward or HP pieces when getting killed too fast.

Battle strategy

World farming

Use fast summons and avoid overbuilding for long fights. Swap in Ward or HP if enemies punch through you.

Dungeons

Start safer than you think, keep Ward ready, and avoid pact plays that expose you for the next floor.

Raids

Build the field, protect important summons, and cash in with pacts only when the trade is favorable.

Towers

Get opening summons established, maintain Ward, and do not sacrifice summons carelessly before dangerous fights.

PvP

You need faster setup, better immunities, and fewer assumptions. Slow perfect plans often fail into pressure.

Common mistakes

  • Ignoring Ward/HP. Summons cannot carry a fight after you are dead.
  • Switching too early and getting frustrated. A class unlock is not a full build.
  • Overusing Blood Pact. Spending summon HP is a real cost.
  • Buying T9 Auriga expecting T10 Grand Summoner Auriga power. Do not confuse a stepping stone with the finished class.
  • Ignoring status immunity. Lost turns can ruin setup.
  • Forgetting setup time. A build that needs several turns must survive those turns first.
