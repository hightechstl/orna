Tower Tips

A short field note for safer tower climbs and fewer run-ending greed checks.

Last updated July 1, 2026

Quick rule

The best tower run is the one you finish. Skip fights that can end the climb unless the reward is worth the risk.

Choose floors carefully

Enemy type, current health, cooldowns, and status risk all matter more in towers than in casual farming.

Build for recovery

Ward, HP, resistances, and status protection are boring until they save the climb.

Leave before greed wins

If the run has already paid out and the next floor looks ugly, taking the win is a real strategy.
