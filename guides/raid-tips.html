Raid Tips

A short field note for getting more useful raid attempts out of the same character.

Last updated July 1, 2026

Quick rule

Survival is damage. A raid attempt that lasts twice as long usually teaches more and deals more total damage.

Open with stability

Start with buffs, Ward, summons, or defensive setup before chasing big hits.

Use debuffs when they matter

Debuffs are strongest when they change the fight, not when they merely fill a turn.

Swap loadouts intentionally

Use a safer raid loadout while learning and a greedier damage loadout once the fight is stable.
